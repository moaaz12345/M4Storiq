Our Mission

M4Storiq was created out of a simple frustration: why do essential PC maintenance tools always come with a hefty price tag or hidden premium tiers? We believe that keeping your personal computer fast, clean, and optimized should be a right, not a luxury.

Developed independently, M4Storiq is built using modern web technologies to provide an elegant, smooth, and blazing fast experience right on your desktop. It scans your drives, identifies storage hogs, and fine-tunes your system to ensure maximum efficiency.

Who is M4Sora?

M4Sora is a digital intelligence agency run by two co-founders, specializing in AI automation, custom LLM solutions, and high-performance websites.

This specific tool, M4Storiq, was primarily developed by co-founder Moaaz Ali as a gift to the community—a way to give back and help people take control of their digital lives without opening their wallets.
